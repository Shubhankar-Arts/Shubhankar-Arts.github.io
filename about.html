About Me

Sanad - Tabla Player

Sanad is a renowned tabla artist known for his profound understanding of rhythm and unparalleled artistry. A celebrated performer with his captivating tabla solos and collaborative performances with some of the most esteemed classical musicians.

With a rich heritage in Indian classical music, Sanad began his journey at a young age and quickly rose to prominence, becoming one of the most sought-after percussionists in the industry. His dedication to the craft and commitment to promoting Indian classical music are evident in every performance.

When not performing, Sanad devotes his time to teaching and mentoring the next generation of tabla artists, ensuring that the legacy of this beautiful art form continues to thrive.
